Final Beta Alpha

IDM241: Allrecipes Final Build

Triggers

  • Hover on/off favorite icon on recipe card
  • Click favorite icon on recipe card
  • Click toast notification's X icon to dismiss it
  • Click on 'Add to collection' button
    • Located on the right side of the toast notification that appears when the user clicks the favorite icon
  • Click X icon to close 'Add to collection' menu

Rules

Hover on favorite icon:

  • Cursor change into pointer
  • Circle becomes 10% bigger
  • Red darkens in color by 10%

Hover off favorite icon:

  • Pointer change into cursor
  • Circle becomes 10% smaller
  • Red darkens in color by 10%

Click on favorite icon (Save):

  • Heart shape becomes filled + scales up 1.25% and then scales down 1.25%
  • Favorite icon pressed = true is saved in backend
  • Toast notification that says "Saved to liked" slides up and expands from bottom of screen, stays for 1 minute, and then shrinks and slides down out of the screen

Click on toast notification's X icon:

  • Toast notification is dismissed and icon fades out

Click on heart icon(Unsave):

  • Heart shape becomes unfilled
  • Heart shape scales down 1.25% and then scales up 1.25%
  • Favorite icon pressed = false is saved in backend
  • Toast notification that says "Removed from liked'" slides up and expands from bottom of screen, stays for 5 seconds, and then shrinks and slides down out of the screen

Hover on 'Add to collection' button

  • Cursor change to pointer
  • Button fills with white, text becomes black

Hover off 'Add to collection' button

  • Pointer change to cursor
  • Button returns to original state (white outline)

Hover on 'X' button on toast notification

  • Cursor change to pointer
  • X scales up 1.25% and turns red

Hover off 'X' button on toast notification

  • Pointer change to cursor
  • X scales down 1.25% and turns white

Click on 'Add to collection' button

  • Menu of possible collections fades in

Click on X icon next to menu

  • Menu of possible collections fades out
